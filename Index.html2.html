What is Wordpress and who is Andy Hernandez?

With a sneak peak of the writer, Andy Hernandez' upcoming comic in 2020 and his weekely blog about the infinite universe.

WordPress.com

WordPress.com is a blogging platform that is owned and operated by Automattic, Inc. It is run on a modified version of WordPress.org, an open source piece of software used by bloggers.

Our Galaxy Blog

Our Galaxy Blog is a website specifically for blogging by Andy Hernandez. He creates a new 300 word blog each Sunday for his Digital Media writing course.

The Wonders of Our Galaxy

The Wonders of the Galaxy is a blog that Andy Hernandez is writing. He talks about a variety of subjects that happen in our massive galaxy. From the moon to what would happen if you were to dive into the center of a black hole.

Ask-Man

Ask-Man is a fictional character created by Andy Hernandez. It's about a mysterious humanoid who destroys but yet saves. The duality of the character and the origin are being kept a secret. The second volume is being worked on at this very moment.

The Death of Ask-Man

The Death of Ask-Man is an American comic created by Andy Hernandez in 2020.
